Game Description

Your ability to remember will be what decides your fate. Hidden within these cards, is the ultimate killing spell, which will automatically end your opponent. Matching the correct fourcards casts Avada Kedavra. Matching two spell cards together will cast that spell and reduce or add the corresponding points from your opponent's health. Different spells reduce different amounts of health. Each witch or wizard begins with 20 points. Game ends when someone runs out of health or when Avada Kedavra is successfully cast.

  • Spells and their results:
    • Avada Kedavra: Automatic kill and ends the game.
    • Expulso: minus 8 points from health
    • Incendio: minus 8 points from health
    • Liberacorpus: gives back points from previous spell
    • Stupefy: minus 2 points from health
    • Rennervate: adds 3 points to health
    • Expelliarmus: minus 2 point from health
    • Finite Incantatum: adds 2 points to health
